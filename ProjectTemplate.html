Gender Constructor 2019

Quick Facts

Language English
Tools Excell
Team Size Me
Is it Available? Yes.
Started May 26, 2019
Last Worked On Jun 03, 2019

This was my first physical game I worked on. For my intr to queer studies class, the final project was open ended, so I decided to double dip and make this for the jam and my final project. I think it could use a little bit of art and card discriptions to make everything look good but I had fun making it. My main goal was to try and show how gender is socially constructed with humor, while making a fun game.
