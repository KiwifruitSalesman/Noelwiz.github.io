What is this Game?

You are a traveling merchant on Klocios, one of the continents in my world. The core gameplay loop consists of traveling from city to city, buying and selling your stuff to make money, and then upgrading your caravan.

Planned Features

  • Choose from one of 2 races with different abilities
  • World Anvil Links for more Lore/information on places and things
  • Upgrades to help you travel faster, carry more, and even sail on the water
  • AI Merchants that also travel around moving goods like you
  • Merchant Guilds and other groups who want to hire you to move stuff around for them
