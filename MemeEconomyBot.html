What is this?

Think of the economy. Now Imagine the stocks are memes, and that you trade them on discord. That is this bot (more or less)!

How do I use this?

Instillation


Download the files from Itch.io above. After that, compile them. Then, go do discord and create a bot user for the bot at Discord's site. Add the bot to your server, and create a text file in the folder you compiled the bot in called "bottoken.txt" and put your bot token from discord's site into the text file. Finally run the bot. Hopefully you can trouble shoot any errors.

Commands

All commands are triggered with $. If a meme's name has a space, it's tag must be used in any commands.

$initialize
Intializes the bot for the server, actually entirely unsessicary.
$startround
Starts a new round of trading.
$endround
Ends a round of trading.
$join
Joins the current round of trading - so you will be paied interest and can buy/sell memes.
$portfolio
Privately messages you your portfolio of memes.
$memeinfo [meme tag]
Sends info about a meme including it's dividends payed per share, tag, and price.
$sell [tag] [amount]
Sells the amount of that meme.
$help
Sends this information.
$trade [meme tag] [amount of meme] [amount of $] @user
Offer to trade an amount of memes for the $amount of money from you to them both numbers can be - so sign matters this is not implemented for jam at all
$playmusic
Will play meme economy theme song. Not implemented.
$pausemusic
Will stops the meme economy theme song. Not yet implemented.$cancilorder [meme tag] [amount]
Deletes an order from your queue this round.
$clearorders
Clears your orders for this round.
$givemequeue
Sends you your order queue maybe.
$getprices
Sends the current market to chat.
