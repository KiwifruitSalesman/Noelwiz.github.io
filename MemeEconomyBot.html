Commands

All commands are triggered with $. If a meme's name has a space, it's tag must be used in any commands.

$initialize
Intializes the bot for the server, actually entirely unsessicary.
$startround
Starts a new round of trading.
$endround
Ends a round of trading.
$join
Joins the current round of trading - so you will be paied interest and can buy/sell memes.
$portfolio
Privately messages you your portfolio of memes.
$memeinfo [meme tag]
Sends info about a meme including it's dividends payed per share, tag, and price.
$sell [tag] [amount]
Sells the amount of that meme.
$help
Sends this information.
$trade [meme tag] [amount of meme] [amount of $] @user
Offer to trade an amount of memes for the $amount of money from you to them both numbers can be - so sign matters this is not implemented for jam at all
$playmusic
Will play meme economy theme song. Not implemented.
$pausemusic
