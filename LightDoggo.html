What is this?

This is a two player game where a ghost dog helps their human through the underworld to try and bring light back to world. !

Current State

Currently the overworld, and first level are complete, the ui generally works, and it's playable.

Other Info

I have no plans to finish this game at this time, and I don't think any of the other people who worked on it do either. I did the music, main menu, and some of the coding. The art and other work, are done by the other team memebers listed in the credits. There are more detailed credits listed in game.

How do I use this?

Instillation


Download the files from Itch.io above. After that, you should be able to unzip the files then run them.

Controlls

Player 1 uses a controller or keyboard to move, and cannot attack. Player 2 uses the mouse to move. There's a dead zone around the dog, and you can click to attack enemies to help Player 1 safely navigate the level. The controlls are also in the game, under the controlls menu.
