Clockwork Mansion

Quick Facts

Language Lua
Tools Love2D
Team Size 5
Is it Available? Yes.
Started March 7th 2020
Last Worked On March 9th 2020

This game was my first experince with Lua but was a ton of fun. NotExplosive was a huge help, introducing us to mob programming (think paired programming but more people), and helped it turn out really nicely. The full team was me, Elliot, Jo, Cullen, and NotExplosive.

In the game you wander a mansion and have to figure out how to save the world. The problem is that you don't know who's trying to end the world, and who is just living their best life. It's supposed to take a few play throughs for you to start to learn what everyone does and how it all fits together so that you can stop it.

Light Doggo

Quick Facts

Language C#
Tools Unity
Team Size 3
Is it Available? Yes.
Started April 22, 2018
Last Worked On September 30, 2018

Light Doggo: Hades has stolen all the light from the world, but luckily your dead dog Spot has come to guide you through the underworld to Hades palace to steal back the light. This is a 2 player game, although it is playable as just 1 player, with partial controller support. One player controlls spot and can shoot enemies while the other player has to make it through the world. I worked on some of the Dialogue UI, and the controlls for the game.

DXM

Quick Facts

Language C#
Tools Unity
Team Size 2
Is it Available? Yes.
Started Feb 24, 2019
Last Worked On Feb 25, 2019

Think of tetris, now imagine what it would be like if gravity rotated periodically, and the blocks were only 3 squares. That is DXM. This one was a bit tough writing our own physics system and collissions to keep the nice grid look. I somewhat succeeded but it needs some work before it's really good. I think both me and Vae I worked with want to fix and get this game to a playable state in the future. I did the coding, while Vae did the music and art for the game.
