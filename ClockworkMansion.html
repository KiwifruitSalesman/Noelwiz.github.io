Clockwork Mansion

Quick Facts

Language Lua
Tools Love2D
Team Size 5
Is it Available? Yes.
Started March 7th 2020
Last Worked On March 9th 2020

This game was my first experince with Lua but was a ton of fun. NotExplosive was a huge help, introducing us to mob programming (think paired programming but more people), and helped it turn out really nicely. The full team was me, Elliot, Jo, Cullen, and NotExplosive.

In the game you wander a mansion and have to figure out how to save the world. The problem is that you don't know who's trying to end the world, and who is just living their best life. It's supposed to take a few play throughs for you to start to learn what everyone does and how it all fits together so that you can stop it.
