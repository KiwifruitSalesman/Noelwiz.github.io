I also did the sound design for the game, which was an interesting second challenge trying to put sound to a soft yarn man.

Schlurbie OST

This music was me trying to practice composing a bit again. I'm taking video game programming about now (a week after the music was written), and I'm planning on composing origional music for that. I was inspired by one of the themes, and our setting: "Help I'm stuck in a Factory Factory". Something about factories reminded me of Melodius Thunk since I played the song in highschool with my highschool band and it does a fantasic job of building a wonderful song out of chaos, like a Factory Factory. Additionally I tried to make my music a little grunge like because factories aren't normally considered clean places, there's a lot of grating and unpleasent noise in my mind. Finally in the second track, I panned the audio a lot to create some effects of the sound moving around the listener and shifting. The game can be found on itch.io over here.
